‚ú¶ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BlockVerse and our platform.

What is BlockVerse and how does it work?

BlockVerse is a comprehensive Web3 platform that enables users to interact with decentralized applications, trade cryptocurrencies, stake tokens, and participate in DeFi protocols. Our platform uses smart contracts to ensure trustless, transparent, and secure transactions.

How do I connect my wallet to BlockVerse?

Simply click the "Connect Wallet" button and choose your preferred wallet provider such as MetaMask, WalletConnect, or Coinbase Wallet. Follow the prompts to authorize the connection, and you're ready to start using the platform.

What are the fees for using BlockVerse?

BlockVerse charges minimal platform fees that are significantly lower than traditional exchanges. Trading fees start at 0.1%, and staking has zero platform fees. Gas fees are determined by the underlying blockchain network.

Is BlockVerse secure and audited?

Yes, security is our top priority. Our smart contracts have been audited by leading security firms including CertiK and OpenZeppelin. We also implement multi-signature wallets, bug bounty programs, and continuous security monitoring.

How can I earn rewards on BlockVerse?

There are multiple ways to earn on BlockVerse: stake $BVT tokens for staking rewards, provide liquidity to earn trading fees, participate in yield farming opportunities, or join our referral program to earn commissions.

What blockchains does BlockVerse support?

BlockVerse currently supports Ethereum, Polygon, Binance Smart Chain, Arbitrum, and Avalanche. We're continuously adding support for additional networks based on community demand and strategic partnerships.
